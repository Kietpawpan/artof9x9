Evaluating the game play qualiy with superhuman AI
Evaluating game play quality with superhuman AI involves comparing a player's moves to those recommended by an AI system that has achieved a level of performance surpassing human experts. This can provide valuable insights into a player's strengths, weaknesses, and decision-making processes.

Best Move Agreement is a metric that quantifies the level of alignment between a player's chosen move in a 9 × 9 Go game and the move deemed best by A Master of Go (Superhuman AI). It's expressed as a percentage, where:
  • 100%: Perfect agreement. The player's move is identical to the master's.
  • 0%: No agreement. The player's move is completely different from the master's.
Move Strength is the impact of each move on the opponent's winning probability compared to the superhuman AI's recommended move
  • A positive Move Strength indicates that a player's moves, on average, improve their chances of winning and decrease their opponent's chances more effectively than the AI.
